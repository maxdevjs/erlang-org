Erlang Workshops

ACM SIGPLAN

PLI (Principles, Logics, and Implementations of High-Level Programming Languages) was a confederation of conferences and workshops aimed at the advancement of high-level programming languages and ICFP (International Conference on Functional Programming) provides a forum for researchers and developers to hear about the latest work on design, implementations, principles, and uses of functional programming.

Beginning in 2001 there have been Erlang workshops co-located or affiliated with the PLI’s and ICFP’s.
